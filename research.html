Research

In my student years, I have specialized in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data Science. My current research lies within the AI4SE framework, that is, leveraging AI for software quality and testing.

The project I am currently working is testing Smart Ecosystems (SES). SES are multifaceted systems that emerge from the composition of independently-operated and autonomous systems with smart functionalities and that evolve over time. The project aims to develop a framework for predicting, identifying, and preventing failures in SES.
