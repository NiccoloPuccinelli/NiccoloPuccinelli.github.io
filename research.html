Research

During my student years, I have specialized in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data Science. My current research lies within the AI4SE framework, that is, leveraging AI for software quality and testing.

I am currently working on testing Smart Human-centric Ecosystems (SHEs). SHEs are multifaceted systems that emerge from the composition of independently-operated and autonomous systems with smart functionalities and that evolve over time. My goal is to develop a framework for predicting, identifying, and preventing failures in SHEs.
