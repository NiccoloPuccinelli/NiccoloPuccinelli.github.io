Bio

I am a PhD student in Informatics at the Software Testing and Analysis Research (STAR) group, supervised by Prof. Mauro Pezzè, at Università della Svizzera italiana (USI) in Switzerland.

I obtained my Bachelor degree in Computer Science from the University of Pisa in 2021, when I successfully defended my thesis titled Benchmarking Reservoir and Recurrent Neural Networks for Human State and Activity Recognition under the supervision of Prof. Davide Bacciu, Prof. Claudio Gallicchio, Prof. Alessio Micheli and Daniele Di Sarli. The work was presented at the International Work-Conference on Artificial Neural Networks (IWANN) 2021.

I obtained my Master degree in Data Science (summa cum laude) from the University of Milano-Bicocca in 2023, when I successfully defended my thesis titled On the Use of Personalized Models for Blood Glucose Concentration Prediction under the supervision of Prof. Paolo Napoletano and Flavio Piccoli. The work was presented at the IEEE 13th International Conference on Consumer Electronics (ICCE) - Berlin.
